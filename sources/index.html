Most recently

I am teacher assistant at Institut de Physique du Globe de Paris. I recently defended my PhD in Seismology in collaboration with Institut de Physique du Globe de Paris and Institut Langevin, ESPCI Paris. At the moment, I am actively looking for a post-doc position.


Research interests

  • Ambient seismic wavefield and its use for structural imaging
  • Continuous monitoring of microseismicity and the Atmosphere-Ocean-Earth system
  • Machine-learning techniques applied to signal identification and classification
  • Array processing of continuous array seismic data
  • Near-real time monitoring of active zones with seismic interferometry
