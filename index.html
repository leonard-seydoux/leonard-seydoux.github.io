I am working in Seismology at Institut de Physique du Globe de Paris. I recently defended my PhD, and I am currently looking for a post-doc position. I will continue to work in Institut de Physique du Globde de Paris until may, 2017.

I am mainly motivated by developping array processing methods useful to analyze the content of continuous seismic data collected by seismic arrays. I am both interested in improving passive Green's function reconstruction from ambient seismic noise in unfavorable contexts, and in signal detection and classification from array seismic data with automatic array-processing methods. Also, I am interested in near-real time monitoring of actives zones with array processing approaches.

I develop algorithms with Python, Matlab, C/C++ and also do High Perfomance Computing with MPI and OpenMP.
